Hello & Welcome to the Official fanpage for Easy Life. Here you will find everything and anything Easy Life related. From updates to social media links. This page was inspired by my love and appreciation for these cheeky east midlands lads. Big Up Leicester City!!

The EZ Lads

From left to right: Sam, Olly, Murray, Jordan and Lewis

Easy Life are an English alternative group formed in 2017 in Leicester. In 2018 Easy Life signed to Island Records. They were placed second on BBC's annual music poll, Sound of 2020. Their mixtape, Junk Food, reached No. 7 on the UK Albums Chart a day after its release.
